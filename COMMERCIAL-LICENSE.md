## Do you need a commercial license?

You do **not** need one if you use unmodified or modified MDTree under the
full terms of the AGPL-3.0, including publishing source code for any network-
accessible modified version.

You **do** need one if you want to:

- Distribute MDTree, or a product built on it, under a different license than
  the AGPL.
- Offer a modified version of MDTree as a hosted or network service without
  releasing your modifications under the AGPL.
- Embed MDTree in proprietary software without triggering the AGPL's
  copyleft obligations.
